What is a Growth Mindset?

A growth mindset is the belief that abilities and intelligence can be developed through dedication and hard work. It’s about embracing challenges, learning from criticism, and persisting in the face of setbacks.

Tips to Build and Maintain a Growth Mindset:

  • View challenges as opportunities to grow.
  • Replace “I can’t” with “I can’t yet.”
  • Celebrate small wins and progress.
  • Learn from feedback and constructive criticism.
  • Surround yourself with people who inspire and challenge you.
  • Remember that no matter the room or how many people are in it no one person has all the answers so never be afraid to speak your truth.
